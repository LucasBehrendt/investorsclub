About Investors Club

Founded in 2018, Investors Club is a vibrant community with engaging and passionate members, located in the south of Sweden. We aim to create a forum where everyone is welcome, be it newcomers who want to learn more about the financial markets, or seasoned veterans with experience to share and exchange. We have a rich variety of members, ranging from young, hungry students with new and exciting ideas, to senior investors with tons of tried and proven strategies.

Our idea is a simple, but effective one. Put talented and passionate people in the same room, and magic happens! We regularly host events, such as dinners and bar meets to get the creative juices flowing. Check out our Events page for upcoming meetups.

Why Join?

Since the founding of Investors Club we have strived to help our members become better investors. Over the years we have evolved to become a rich, diverse community with an incredibly strong sense of cohesion. Our members frequently exchange valuable and enriching experiences, while creating new ones together.

Whether you are looking for some inspiration and tips to take your investing to the next level, or just looking to find some friends to grab a beer with, Investors Club is the place for you!
