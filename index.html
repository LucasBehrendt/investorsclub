About Investors Club

Founded in 2011, Investors Club is a vibrant community with engaging and passionate members, located in the south of Sweden. We aim to create a forum where everyone is welcome, be it newcomers who want to learn more about the financial markets, or seasoned veterans with experience to share and exchange. We have a rich variety of members, ranging from young, hungry students with new and exciting ideas, to senior investors with tons of tried and proven strategies.

Our idea is a simple, but effective one. Put talented and passionate people in the same room, and magic happens! We regularly host events, such as dinners and bar meets to get the creative juices flowing. Check out our Events page for upcoming meetups.
